One designer, five disciplines

I'm a designer based in Haifa working across product, brand, web, wayfinding and motion. I started in physical experience design — signage systems and spatial information architecture — and brought those principles into everything I do digitally.

What I care about most: making things legible. Whether it's a hospital with 800 signs, an app with 50 flows, or a new brand with zero recognition — the problem is the same. How do people know where they are and what to do next?

Currently at Raya Galboa Design Studio, previously at Shnorkel Studio (BDO Digital) — and teaching Figma to the next generation of designers.
